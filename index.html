About Me

My Professional Journey

Hi there! I'm Alexandra, a Product Owner with 2 years of PO experience built on 8 years of UX research expertise. I translate business needs into system requirements and work closely with engineering teams.

My journey started as a UX Designer and front-end developer, then evolved into UX Research where I spent years helping products discover true user needs. Now, as a Product Owner, I bridge the gap between operational needs and technical solutions.

Currently, I'm diving deep into technical literacy — working hands-on with API integrations, process automation, and AI-driven products. I'm passionate about understanding the technical side: writing specs, vibe coding, and exploring how AI can enhance product development.

My aspiration is to be a Product Owner who truly speaks the language of both users and engineers — combining deep UX insights with technical understanding to build products that scale.
